# iHomeNerd: The AI Guard Dog (Vision Reboot)

**Status:** Reboot (Dec 2025)
**Core Concept:** Local-First Home Intelligence
**Hardware:** OrangePi 5 Plus | Mac Studio

---

## 1. The Pivot
Originally a "Smart Home Hub" concept, `iHomeNerd` is pivoting to a focused **"Local Intelligence Appliance"**.
We are not competing with Home Assistant. We are building the **"Brain"** that Home Assistant talks to.

## 2. Core Pillars
1.  **Privacy:** No video leaves the house. Cloud is for text summaries only.
2.  **Intelligence:** Not just "Motion Detected". "The dog is eating the trash."
3.  **Action:** PTZ Control, Audio Warnings, Automatic Lighting.

## 3. Product Roadmap

### Phase 1: Vacation Mode (The MVP)
*   **Hardware:** OrangePi 5 Plus.
*   **Feature:** Connects to RTSP streams. Summarizes daily activity. Emails you.
*   **Why:** Solving the "Notification Fatigue" of Ring/Nest.

### Phase 2: Construction Mode (The Upsell)
*   **Hardware:** Mac Studio.
*   **Feature:** Time-Lapse construction monitoring, worker safety, material tracking.
*   **Why:** High-value asset protection.

### Phase 3: Active Guard (The Future)
*   **Hardware:** Any PTZ Camera.
*   **Feature:** Autonomous tracking ("Follow the person walking up the driveway").
*   **Why:** Proactive deterrence.
